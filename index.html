About

Arrubio is a Moroccan indie artist based in Antwerp, blending alternative R&B, indie rock and soul into a warm, atmospheric sound. His music is defined by rich synth textures, layered guitar work and a focus on emotion and mood.

Starting out with GarageBand at a young age, he developed a production style where instrumentation is used as a tool to shape feeling and space. Together with his band, Arrubio delivers intimate yet energetic live performances, balancing groove, vulnerability and cinematic atmosphere.

Alternative R&B rooted in warmth, rhythm and atmosphere.
